Reatta

This song was produced with real sound samples from a Buick Reatta. This song is inspired by retro wave, and related musical genres. It aims to feel like a song you would blast while cornering in an 80's sport coupé. There's a red light in the middle of the song - can you feel it?

Flying Jib Cutter

Robbed

Sonically inspired by what I remember conversations in dreams sounding like after I wake up. Using various effects and voice samples, I build up a layered blanket of sound that feels unsettling and distant. At the end, I downsample the sound to represent waking up, and the dissolving of the specifics of the dream.

Trashcant

A chiptune sketch with a large intro, with a following "action" theme. Inspired by the many intro screens and level themes of video games I Love.

Pacific Trash Vortex

A longer sketch that has varying levels of energy and speed. After building out all the stems, I mixed and matched them to suit varying energy levels. Heavy inspiration from Daft Punk's live shows, where they frequently remix their studio songs by playing with tempo and energy to control the audience reaction.

ICH R U

This is a short cover of ICH R U by Boyz Noize. I sampled the vocals from the song and recreated some of the same sythesizer sounds from scratch. This practice helped me develop my active listening skills.
