Originally from Asheville, North Carolina, Lucas Rumney currently lives in the Bay Area while working as a Software Engineer for Apple. He has a Bachelors in Computer Engineering from North Carolina State University. Lucas is constantly honing his knowledge of Mixed-Reality Design Principles and Audio Production by spending his time producing various projects. He plays saxophone, guitar, and electronic synthesizer. He enjoys collecting and emulating games from all periods of video game history. He also enjoys hiking, photography, and traveling.
